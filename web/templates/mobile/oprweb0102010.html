Subscription Privacy Notice

The events included will be the next 9 months and the last 3 months. Once you have subscribed your Calendar application will check for updates when the Calendar application is accessed. Subscriptions that include patient events will include patient information. As always, you must respect patients privacy. Please ensure that you have appropriate device security measures in place if you are subscribing to such events. By using these services you agree to abide by the hospitals policies in relation to patient privacy and to ensure that your device is properly secured. If you have any querys about this please contact ICT Services.
